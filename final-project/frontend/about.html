About Us

Pixel Puranas is an innovative platform that brings the history of India to life using the power of Generative AI. Our mission is to make history accessible, engaging, and fun for everyone. We believe that history should be a source of inspiration and pride, not a dry and dusty subject.

Features

AI-Driven Storytelling

Enter a prompt about an Indian historical event or figure, and let AI create engaging, accurate, and visually stunning content.

Visual Learning

Experience history through vivid visuals, animations, and natural audio narration, for those who prefer auditory learning.

Interactive Learning

Ask questions and explore topics deeply using our chatbot powered by advanced NLP models.
